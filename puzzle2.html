Puzzle1 GearBall
Wade Zengel
Enter the number of random turns you want into the textbox and then click "Randomize" to generate a ball.
The buttons on the right side of the page let you directly interact with the gear ball.
Turning any set will turn its lowercase set in the opposite direction.
These buttons use the blue and yellow square for right and left turns for sets B and C.
By clicking the solve button a solution will be displayed.
It is recommended to only randomize a number of moves between 1 - 5 for time purposes.
A fast computer can do up to 7 randomized moves.
